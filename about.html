Company Overview

Natural Beeswax Supply from Hebei, China

Hebei Cera Rica Industrial and Trade Co., Ltd. is engaged in the production and export of natural beeswax products. Based in Hebei Province, China, we focus on supplying practical beeswax solutions for overseas distributors, manufacturers, brands, and wholesale buyers.

Our main product direction includes yellow beeswax, white beeswax, and refined beeswax products in forms suitable for bulk inquiry and export discussion, including block, slab, pellet, and flake options.

We keep our public presentation simple and commercially useful: enough information for a buyer to understand our product direction, while exact requirements can be discussed according to application, destination market, quantity, and packaging needs.
